About

Scrappy is a portal made by the students of Adi Shankara Institute of Engineering and Technology, Kalady which aims to get rid of all the scraps sitting at home. The Portal can be accessed from Smartphones as well as Computers.

With Scrappy, the users can post ads and let the dealers bid and get the best price for their scraps. The dealers have the adavantage of avoiding the trouble to every house and ask whether they have scraps. The dealer can bid and if they bid the highest, they get the location and can go and get the scraps.
